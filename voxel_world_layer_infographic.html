🔍 Key Insight

Only 3-6 blocks of vertical space between sea level (Y: 124) and typical grassland surface (Y: ~128) provides dry, explorable cave space. This narrow band makes cave exploration in grasslands extremely limited compared to other biomes. Most cave systems below sea level are completely flooded and inaccessible.
